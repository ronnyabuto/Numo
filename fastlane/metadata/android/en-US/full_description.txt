Numo is a free and open-source Android Point-of-Sale application that allows merchants to receive Bitcoin payments through Lightning or Cashu ecash tap-to-pay.

The application acts as a simple point-of-sale terminal for receiving Bitcoin. When a customer approaches their phone with a supporting wallet, the app receives the payment over NFC.

How It Works:
The merchant enters the desired amount using the application's keypad. When they press "Charge", a payment screen appears. For ecash payments, a type-4 forum tag is emulated with a Cashu Payment Request. The payer's wallet reads the contents via NFC and writes a Cashu token back to the terminal, which Numo immediately processes. 

Numo also supports direct Lightning payments. The merchant can switch to the "Lightning" tab to generate a fresh BOLT11 invoice, which is displayed as a QR code and transmitted over NFC for compatible wallets.

Features:
- Receive Bitcoin via Lightning BOLT11 invoices
- Tap-to-pay with Cashu ecash and NDEF protocol
- Merchant item catalogs with pre-selectable items
- Merchant item baskets that can be persisted
- Payment history and wallet integration
- Withdraw to self-custody or Lightning address
- Automatic withdrawal after threshold is reached

Important: This application is a Point-of-Sale terminal. It is intended to receive payments and immediately generate redemption tokens.
